Consider a displacement field with the form:

\begin{equation*}
\hat{\bm{x}}(x,y,z)=\bm{x}(x)+\bm{\theta}(x) \times \bm{r}(y,z)
%+\left.\bm{\alpha}(x)\cdot\bm{\varphi}(y,z)\right.\; \mathbf{i}
\end{equation*}
where the rotation \(\bm\theta\) is:
\begin{equation}
\bm{\theta} = \vartheta \FrameBasis  + \FrameBasis \times \bar{\bm{u}}^{\prime}
\end{equation}
where \(\bar{\bm u} = u_\alpha\FrameBasis_{\alpha}\) are the plane displacements of the centerline.

\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
\ShearStrain &= \FrameBasis \otimes\FrameBasis  ~ \bm{u}^{\prime} - \FrameBasis  \\
\CurveStrain &= \vartheta^{\prime} \FrameBasis  + \FrameBasis \times\bar{\bm{u}}^{\prime\prime}
\end{aligned}
\label{eq:basic-euler-strains}
\end{equation}
