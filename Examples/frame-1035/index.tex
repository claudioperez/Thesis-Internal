A simply supported span of length $L = 6\unit{\meter}$ is subjected to constant flexure (\cref{fig:1035-model}). 
A closed form solution for the critical buckling moment is \citep[(7.22)]{trahair1993flexuraltorsional}:
$$
\begin{aligned}
M_{\mathrm{cr}} & =\frac{\pi}{L}\sqrt{E I_y G J} \left[\left(1+\frac{\pi^2 \beta_x^2 E I_y}{4L^2 G \Jo}\right)^{1 / 2}+\frac{\pi \beta_x}{2 L}\left(\frac{E I_y}{G \Jo}\right)^{1 / 2}\right] .
\end{aligned}
\qquad\text{with}\qquad 
\beta_x = \frac{1}{I_x}\int y (x^2 + y^2)\dA - 2y_0
$$

The span is discretized by 10 elements. 
\cite{du2021threedimensionala} obtained similar results with 20 elements and \cite{kitipornchai1986lateral} with 30 elements.
