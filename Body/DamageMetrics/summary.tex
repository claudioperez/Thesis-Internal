\cref{part:predictors} is targeted at practicing engineers and also researchers. 
The concept of a \gls{predictor} is developed in detail,
which provides the fundamental abstraction through which structural health
monitoring is performed.
The predictors that perform bridge response simulation are \glspl{predictor-t1}. They consist of a family of structural analysis models. The general process for constructing a \gls{predictor-t1} is described in \cref{ch:predictor_t1}. The predictors that perform data-driven bridge dynamics identification are \glspl{predictor-t2}. They consist of a family of system identification procedures. A practical guide to viewing, configuring, and interpreting the results of \glspl{predictor-t2} is provided in \cref{ch:predictor_t2}.
