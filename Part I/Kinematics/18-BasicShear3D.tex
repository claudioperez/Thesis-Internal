The linearization of \eqref{eq:finite-alignment-field} is:
\begin{equation*}
\hat{\bm{x}}(x,y,z)=\bm{x}(x)+\bm{\theta}(x) \times \bm{r}(y,z)
+\bm{\Phi}(y,z)\bm{\alpha}(x)
\end{equation*}

In terms of \(\bm{u}\triangleq\bm{x}- \reflenx\,\FrameBasis\) the linearized generalized strains are:
\begin{SaveEquation}{eq:gam-kap-linear}
\bm{\gamma}=\bm{u}'+\FrameBasis\times\bm\theta,\qquad
\bm{\kappa}=\bm\theta'.
\end{SaveEquation}

\begin{equation}
\bm{e}= 
\begin{pmatrix}
\epsilon \\ 
\gamma_y \\
\gamma_z \\
\Twist \\
\kappa_z \\ 
\kappa_y \\ 
\end{pmatrix} =
\begin{pmatrix}
u_x^{\prime} \\ 
\left(u_y^{\prime}-\theta_z\right) \\ 
\left(u_z^{\prime}+\theta_y\right) \\
\theta_x^{\prime} \\ 
\theta_y^{\prime} \\
\theta_z^{\prime} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{equation}
