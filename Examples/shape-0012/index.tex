
This example investigates the ambiguous nature of shear flexibility in Saint~Venant's solution, and demonstrates correspondence of the energetic and geometric traces of \cref{sec:timo}. 
Furthermore, the significance of Poisson's ratio is illustrated, which is often neglected by beam formulations incorporating warping \citep{elfatmi2007nonuniform,saritas2004modeling}.

\subsubsection{Hollow Rectangle}\label{sec:frame-0012/H}

\Cref{fig:0012-h} plots the eigenvalues of the shear correction matrix \eqref{eq:shear-correction} for the energetic and geometric traces, as well as the following equation \citep{cowper1966shear}:
\begin{equation*}
\kappa = \frac{10(1+\nu)(1+3 m)^2}{\left(12+72 m+150 m^2+90 m^3\right)+\nu\left(11+66 m+135 m^2+90 m^3\right)+10 n^2\left((3+\nu) m+3 m^2\right)}
\end{equation*}
where \(m=\frac{b t_1}{d t_2}\) and \(n=\frac{b}{d}\).
