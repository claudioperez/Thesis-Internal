\subparagraph{Context.}
This example simulates Test~5 from the experimental program by  \cite{farwell1969nonuniform,pandit1970discussion}.


\subparagraph{Setup.} 
The span in \cref{fig:frame-3011} is subjected to a concentrated torque \(\TwistRxn\) at midspan (\(\reflenx=L/2\)). 
The section is a wide flange shape with dimensions:
$$
\begin{gathered}
b_f=0.1509 ~\unit{\meter} \\
d  =0.1524 ~\unit{\meter} \\
t_w=0.008 ~\unit{\meter} \\
t_f=0.0122 ~\unit{\meter}
\end{gathered}
$$
