\subsubsection{Equilibrium}

The adjoint of the operator \(\mathbb{B}^*\) in \eqref{eq:delta-shear-Bstar} is
\begin{equation}
\mathbb{B} 
= -\begin{bmatrix}
  \mathbf{1}\partial & & \\
& \mathbf{1}\partial & -\mathbf{1}
\end{bmatrix}
\label{eq:delta-shear-Bequi}
\end{equation}
The equilibrium equations then follow:
\begin{equation*}
\begin{aligned}
-\mathbb{B}\bm{A}^{\mathrm{t}} \bm{s}
&= 
\begin{bmatrix}
\mathbf{1}\partial & & \\
 & \mathbf{1}\partial & -\mathbf{1}
\end{bmatrix}
\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
\mathbf{1} + \bm{\theta}^{\times}& \mathbf{0} \\
\mathbf{0} & \mathbf{1} + \frac{1}{2}\bm{\theta}^{\times} \\
{\bm{x}}^{\prime~\times} - \frac{1}{2}\left.\bm{\theta}^{\times}\,\FrameBasis^{\times}\right. & -\frac{1}{2}\bm{\theta}^{\prime \times} 
\end{array}\right] \begin{pmatrix}
\ShearResult \\ \CurveResult
\end{pmatrix} \\
%
% &= \begin{bmatrix}
% \mathbf{1}\partial & & \\
%  & \mathbf{1}\partial & -\mathbf{1}
% \end{bmatrix}
% \begin{pmatrix}
% (\mathbf{1}+\bm{\theta}^\times)\ShearResult \\
% (\mathbf{1}+\frac{1}{2}\bm{\theta}^{\times})\CurveResult \\
% {\bm{x}}^{\prime} {\times} \ShearResult - \frac{1}{2} \bm{\theta} {\times}\,(\FrameBasis  {\times}  \ShearResult) -\frac{1}{2}\bm{\theta}^{\prime } \times \CurveResult
% \end{pmatrix} \\
&= \begin{array}{l}
\ShearResult' + \bm{\theta}'\times\ShearResult \\
\CurveResult' +  \bm{\theta}' \times \CurveResult + \bm{x}'\times\ShearResult - \frac{1}{2} \bm{\theta}\times(\FrameBasis \times\ShearResult)
\end{array}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation*}
